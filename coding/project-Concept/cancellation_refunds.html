1. Cancellation Policy

Customers can request a cancellation of their service subscription or order within 24 hours of purchase. To cancel a subscription, please log in to your account or contact our support team.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of the approved cancellation request. We offer refunds in the following scenarios:

  • Services were not rendered as described.
  • Subscription was cancelled within the trial period.
  • Technical issues prevented access to purchased features.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Certain items or services may be non-refundable, including:

  • One-time setup fees.
  • Custom development work that has already commenced.
  • Partial usage of subscription periods.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cancellation and Refunds Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
