Last Updated: December 17, 2024

1. Digital Delivery

Most of our products are digital services or software licenses. These are delivered electronically via email or through the user portal immediately upon purchase confirmation.

2. Physical Goods Shipping

If applicable, for physical hardware or merchandise:

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
  • Shipping Methods: We offer Standard (5-7 days) and Express (2-3 days) shipping.
  • International Shipping: Available to select countries. Custom duties may apply.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number to monitor your delivery status.

4. Lost or Damaged Items

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact our support team immediately for a replacement or refund.
